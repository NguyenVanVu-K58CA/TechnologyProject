Key features

  • Cambridge IELTS 1 contains 4 Academic tests from test 1 to test 4.
    • Listening

      4 sections, around 40 questions 30 minutes + transfer tiem, answers and audioscripts can be viewed after submit the tests

    • Reading:

      3 sections, around 40 questions 60 minutes, answers can be viewed after submit the tests

    • Writing:

      2 tasks 60 minutes (Contains question only, currently cannot be tested online)

    • Speaking:

      10 to 15 minutes (Contains questions only, currently cannot be tested online)

    • Total test time:

      2 hours 45 minutes

  • For Listening and Reading tests you can get your score instantly after submit your answers, no more wasted time to look up answers manually.
